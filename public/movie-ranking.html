The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Social Network (2010)

A gripping story about the creation of Facebook, showcasing innovation, coding, and the challenges of turning an idea into a global platform. Perfect if you enjoy tech-driven stories and entrepreneurship.

Interstellar (2014)

A visually stunning sci-fi masterpiece that blends space exploration with science, AI, and human resilience. It’s thought-provoking and deeply inspiring if you like exploring the “what ifs” of technology and humanity.

The Imitation Game (2014)

Based on the true story of Alan Turing, it highlights the power of algorithms, codebreaking, and intelligence in shaping history. A must-watch if you’re into AI origins and problem-solving brilliance.
