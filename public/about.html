About Me

I’m Anish Mahapatra, a 3rd-year undergraduate student at Manipal University Jaipur, passionate about artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data-driven problem solving. Over the past few years, I’ve been exploring how intelligent systems can be applied to real-world challenges, from prediction models to automation tools, while building a strong foundation in software development and algorithms.

These experiences have sharpened my organizational, leadership, and teamwork skills, alongside my technical journey.

On GitHub, you’ll find my experiments, projects, and learnings ranging from AI/ML research projects to hands-on coding explorations. I see this space as both a personal learning log and a way to collaborate with others who share similar interests. Always eager to learn, build, and share, I’m working toward creating impactful solutions while continuously growing as a developer and researcher.
